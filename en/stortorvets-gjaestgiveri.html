Stortorvets Gjæstgiveri - Website in Wordpress.

One of the oldest restaurants in Oslo, Stortorvets Gjæstgiveri, wanted to update their website to reflect their new menu, which is a modern take on traditional Norwegian food. They wanted a much lighter color palette compared to their old site and to lean heavy on the photos by Lill Øyunn Photography. It also had to be available in both Norwegian and English. The site is built with Wordpress using a custom made theme.

  • Year: 2015–2016
  • Role: Designer & Developer
