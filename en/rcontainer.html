Rcontainer - Website with bespoke web shop.

Rcontainer AS is a supplier of container and module projects in Norway. Together with NXT, who supplied the design, and Wiggen AS, the brief was to develop a new website with a bespoke web shop where they could receive rental or purchase inquiries for all of their products. Only within months after launch, their revenue had significantly increased and in 2015 they reported sales for 57 million NOK, approx 6.6 million USD, or 6 million EUR. When the site was built, their inventory contained close to 900 products. The site, including the inventory, was translated to English for their international clients. The operation of the site was transferred to another entity in 2015.

  • Year: 2013–2014
  • Role: Developer
