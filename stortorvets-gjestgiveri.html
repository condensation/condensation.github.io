Stortorvets Gjæstgiveri

www.stortorvets-gjestgiveri.no

Website

2015 - 2016

The restaurant Stortorvets Gjæstgiveri in central Oslo wanted their website uptated to reflect their new menu, a more modern take on the traditional norwegian food they serve.

They also wanted it to be a bit lighter in the color scheme and to lean heavy on the photos of the food and the building. The site had to be available in Norwegian and English.

I designed and built the site using a custom Wordpress theme based on Zurb Fountation 5 and used WPML to translate the site.
