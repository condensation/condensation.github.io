Website

2013 - 2014

I was assigned to design and develop a new website in Wordpress using a bespoke Woocommerce solution to receive inqueries for the clients product catalogue, containing almost 900 products at the time.

The website was launched in Norwegian and English.

The design and visual identity was provided by NXT.

My role was to build the website and implement the design as specified by the digital agency and populate it with content from all of their producers.

The site has been maintained by another agency since last summer.
